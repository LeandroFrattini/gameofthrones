Game of Thrones - A song of Ice and Fire

Game of Thrones, titled in Spanish "Juego de Tronos, is an American medieval fantasy television series created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss for the HBO network. It is based on the A Song of Ice and Fire series of novels by writer George R. R. Martin and its plot centers on the violent dynamic struggles between various noble families for control of the Iron Throne of the continent of Westeros. The series was released on April 17, 2011, in the United States, on May 8 in Latin America, and a day later in Spain through Canal +. As of July 2012, the series premiered, openly, through Antena 3. The final episode of the series was broadcast on May 19, 2019. On October 29, 2019, it was announced that HBO had ordered the recording of a prequel series titled House of the Dragon, based on the book Fire and Blood by writer George R. R. Martin.

Game of Thrones follows the multiple storylines of A Song of Ice and Fire. The majority of the story takes place in Westeros, a fictional continent where seasons can last for years, and focuses on the violent dynastic struggles that erupt between various noble families for control of the Iron Throne. The first season is based on the first novel in the saga, Game of Thrones, which also gives the series its name. Its plot takes place fifteen years after the civil war known as Robert's Rebellion or the Usurper's War, in which Robert I Baratheon expelled the Targaryens from the Iron Throne and proclaimed himself king of the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros. Eddard "Ned" Stark, Lord of Winterfell, is forced to accept the position of Hand of the King, for fear of rejecting the offer and the safety of his children being affected, and to discover the true reason for the death of his son. predecessor, Jon Arryn, Lord of House Arryn. Once in King's Landing, the capital of the Seven Kingdoms, Ned will be involved in a convoluted web of secrets. Meanwhile, across the sea, the exiled heir to the throne, Viserys Targaryen, marries his sister Daenerys to the leader of the Dothraki, Khal Drogo, to form an alliance that will allow him to reclaim the Iron Throne. In the north, as a third plot line, an immense Wall guarded by the Night's Watch separates the continent of Westeros from the foreign territories, in which a series of strange events begin to take place, increased by the arrival of winter.
